Adobo Tacos

Description

This recipe is one that I made for an anniversary with my partner that I grabbed from the Bon Appetit website. The official recipe can be found here

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • 1 lb. mixed mushrooms
  • 2 Tbsp. avocado oil
  • 2 Tbsp. lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp. dried oregano
  • 1 Tbsp. onion powder
  • 2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 2 tsp. coconut sugar or organic cane sugar
  • 2 tsp. Diamond Crystal or 1¼ tsp. Morton kosher salt
  • ½ tsp. cayenne pepper
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Radishes
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa
  • Halved Limes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Toss the mushrooms into the spices
  3. Oven for 20ish minutes check to make sure they are crispy
  4. Warm your tortillas
  5. Plate with fresh toppings
