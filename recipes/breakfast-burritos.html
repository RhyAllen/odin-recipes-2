Breakfast Burritos

Description

This recipe is one that my partner and I made one for our first cooked breakfast together

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • 3 eggs each
  • Oil or Cooking Spray
  • Avocado
  • Cheese
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Spinach
  • Chipotle Tabasco
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Spray pan and heat to medium-low heat
  2. Crack three eggs into a cup with spices of your choosing
  3. Beat the eggs and toss them into the pan
  4. Gradually move them throughout the pan until properly scrambled
  5. Warm your tortillas
  6. Cut your avocado (carefully)
  7. Air fry your spinach
  8. Plate with fresh toppings
