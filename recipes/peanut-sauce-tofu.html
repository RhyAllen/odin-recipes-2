Peanut Sauce Tofu

Description

This recipe is what I'm currently cooking at this exact moment.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Extra-firm tofu
  • Broccoli
  • Store bought peanut sauce
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sriracha Sauce

Steps

  1. Press your tofu at least 2 hours in advance
  2. Approximately 40 minutes before you expect to eat cook your rice
  3. Once your tofu is pressed cut it into uniform cubes of your preferred size
  4. Airfry your tofu for 12-15 minutes
  5. After airfry your broccoli for 12-15 minutes
  6. While your broccoli is airfrying mix a heaping spoonful of peanut butter into a bowl with your peanut sauce and add soy sauce and sriracha to taste
  7. Pour this into a simmering pan and toss your tofu and broccoli in it
  8. Once warmed plate over a bowl of rice
